Characters

Builder

Navigating the hazardous terrain, the Builder uses their extra steps to propel players forward for a price. By spinning the roulette, players invest in the Builder's skills to overcome obstacles, outpace rivals, and inch closer to the coveted victory. Builder are great at their work.

Mime

With the power to replicate the abilities of others, the Mime becomes a valuable asset for those seeking strategic advantages. Players can pay the Mime to access their ability services to copy the moves of their adversaries, weaving a web of deception across the board.

Doctor

As the guardian of health and survival, the Doctor offers crucial services for a fee. Players pay the Doctor, securing shields or revival, ensuring their resilience against the challenges and deceitful tactics of opponents. Doctor can also shield themselves to avoid any injury or any misshapen

Murderer

In a realm where trust is scarce, the Murderer offers a deadly advantage. Players spin the roulette to pay the Murderer, eliminating rivals strategically and paving the way to triumph. However, alliances can crumble in the face of deceit. Murderer has low vision.

Yeti

Activated when players land on the Yeti spawn point, the Yeti operates independently, adding an unpredictable element to the game. Players must spin the roulette to determine their next move, adjusting strategies as they encounter the enigmatic creature and face unforeseen consequences.
